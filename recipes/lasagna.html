Classic Lasagna: A Hearty and Flavorful Italian Favorite

Lasagna, a beloved Italian dish, is a hearty and comforting meal perfect for gatherings or cozy family dinners. Layered with tender lasagna noodles, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and a generous filling of seasoned ground meat, this dish offers a symphony of flavors and textures that tantalize the taste buds. Each bite is a delightful combination of savory meat, velvety cheese, and aromatic herbs, creating a culinary experience that is both satisfying and indulgent.

As the lasagna bakes in the oven, the layers meld together, with the cheese bubbling and turning golden brown on top, while the sauce permeates through the noodles, infusing them with its robust flavor. When served piping hot, the lasagna is a sight to behold, its fragrant aroma wafting through the air, enticing all who catch a whiff. Whether enjoyed fresh from the oven or reheated as leftovers, this classic lasagna is sure to please even the most discerning palates, making it a timeless favorite for any occasion.

Ingredients needed:
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown ground beef or Italian sausage in a skillet.
  4. Add chopped onion and minced garlic to skillet; cook until softened.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce and Italian seasoning; simmer.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta, Parmesan, and beaten egg.
  7. Layer lasagna noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  8. Repeat layers, ending with a layer of sauce and shredded mozzarella.
  9. Cover with foil; bake for 25-30 minutes.
  10. Remove foil; bake uncovered until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  11. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  12. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
  13. Slice and enjoy your homemade lasagna!
