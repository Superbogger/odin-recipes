Ingredients needed:
  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions)
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for spice)
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Transfer dough to a greased pizza pan or baking sheet.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over dough.
  5. Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Add desired toppings.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, and red pepper flakes.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.
  10. Enjoy your classic homemade pizza!
